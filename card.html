The Manul Cat

The Manul Cat or Pallas' Cat, is a small wild cat with a broad but fragmented distribution in the grasslands and montane steppes of Central Asia.

  • Mass: 3 kg
  • Length: 57 cm
  • Gestation period: 76 days
  • Conservation Status: Near Threatened

Manuls are distinguished by having round pupils, short legs, and a flat-looking face with wide-set ears that can create some of the funniest expressions in the feline kingdom.
